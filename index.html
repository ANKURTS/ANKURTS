My Education

Embarking on my educational odyssey at JSS Public School, I laid the initial bricks of my academic foundation. The pivotal transition to DPS in the fifth grade broadened my horizons and introduced me to diverse learning methodologies. However, the educational landscape underwent a profound transformation with the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, necessitating an abrupt shift to virtual learning environments. Undeterred, I navigated through these challenging times, adapting and embracing the new normal Subsequently, my academic journey led me to RV PU College, where I engaged in an intellectually stimulating two-year Pre-University Course specializing in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology. This immersive experience not only honed my analytical skills but also instilled a passion for the sciences. Currently enrolled at RV University, I am fervently pursuing a BTech (Hons) in Computer Science and Engineering. This program aligns with my intrinsic curiosity for technology and its transformative potential. The journey through various educational institutions has not only equipped me with a robust academic foundation but has also cultivated resilience and adaptability, traits essential for navigating the dynamic landscape of today's world. My endeavors at RV University symbolize my dedication to advancing my knowledge and skills in the ever-evolving realm of computer science and engineering, fostering a holistic approach to learning and personal development.
